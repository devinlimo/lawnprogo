4. Technology & Future Vision

Technology Stack (Considerations)

  • **Frontend:** Modern JavaScript framework (e.g., React, Vue.js)
  • **Backend:** Robust framework (e.g., Node.js, Python/Django)
  • **Database:** Relational (e.g., PostgreSQL) & potentially NoSQL
  • **Cloud Hosting:** AWS, GCP, or Azure
  • **Key Integrations:** Payment Gateway, Mapping/GIS, Email/SMS, Analytics

Refer to Section 7 of the Technical Requirements document for more details.

Future Considerations (Post-MVP)

  • Native Mobile Apps (iOS & Android)
  • Advanced Provider Analytics & Business Tools
  • Customer Subscription Models
  • AI-Powered Recommendations / Quoting
  • Expansion to New Service Verticals & Geographies

Refer to Section 8 of the Technical Requirements document for more details.
